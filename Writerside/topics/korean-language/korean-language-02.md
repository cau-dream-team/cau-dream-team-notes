# 02. Greeting and Introducing

## Present Ending Form

> `아요`, `어요`, and `해요`

### `아` and `오` {id="kr_0"}

| Dictionary Form | Present Form |
|----------------:|:-------------|
|            `아다` | `아요`         |
|            `앙다` | `앙아요`        |
|            `오다` | `와요`         |

### `우`, `어`, `으`, and `이` {id="kr_1"}

| Dictionary Form | Present Form |
|----------------:|:-------------|
|            `엉다` | `엉어요`        |
|            `잉다` | `잉어요`        |
|            `위다` | `위어요`        |
|            `우다` | `워요`         |
|            `이다` | `여요`         |

### `하`

| Dictionary Form | Present Form |
|----------------:|:-------------|
|            `하다` | `해요`         |

### Irregular

| Dictionary Form | Present Form |
|----------------:|:-------------|
|            `듣다` | `들다`         |

## Past Ending Form

> `았어요`, `었어요`, and `했어요`
